Facts about The Moon

  • It takes approximately 3 days to travel to the Moon from Earth.
  • There are 8 total moon phases. They are waxing cresent, first quarter, waxing gibbous, full moon, waning gibbous, third quarter, waning cresent, and new moon.
  • The moon is responsible for causing high tides and low tides due to its gravitational. This causes Earth's water to bulge towards the Moon.
  • The first footprints made from the Apollo 11 mission, which was 56 years ago, is still there to this day.
