About Me

Hello, my name is Kiseoung Shim. I am Korean, born and raised in Cambodia. I'm the 3rd youngest among my siblings. My mother is Cambodian and my father is Korean. Which makes me Korean-Cambodian. I speak a total of 3 languages which are, Khmer, Korean, and English. I've lived in Cambodia for the majority of my life so coming to Canada is an incredible step forward in my life.

I plan to study IMD here, hopefully graduate, and continue my studies in Korea. By the end of my studying journey, I hope to return back to my home country and help my family business with the knowledge and skills in order to give back to the family with gratitude.
